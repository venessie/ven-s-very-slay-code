All About Pochacco!~;

Pochacco is a Sanrio Charcter who is a dog, born on 1989, Feburay 29, which for your information, is an imaginary date, having to be the closest leap years were either 1988 or 1992. Pochacco was originated from a similar-look-like charcter called Snoopy.

Pochacco's favourite food is Mint and Chocolate, and Banana Ice Cream. His favourite color is green, and he was born in Uguisu Yokocho, but now lives in Fuwa Fuwa Town. His favourite sport is badminton, and he is known as "The Yorimichi Dog"

You may know his duck friend, who is called Popple. Popple is a duck charcter from Sanrio. Research says that Popple is Pochacco's swimming coach, and Popple tends to waddle rather than walk.
